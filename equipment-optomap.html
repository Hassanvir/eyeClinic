Our Technology

Optos OPTOMAP

Ultra-widefield retinal imaging — see more, detect more, protect more.

What is it?

The only true 200° retinal image

Traditional retinal imaging devices capture only a small portion of the eye's interior. The Optos OPTOMAP is the only clinically validated, ultra-widefield retinal imaging system that captures 82% — or 200° — of the retina in a single capture, with potential expansion to 97% using auto-montage functionality.

Unlike conventional cameras that require dilation drops, the OPTOMAP uses low-powered laser wavelengths, making the exam faster, more comfortable, and suitable for patients of all ages.

How It Works

Multi-wavelength laser imaging

Green Laser

Examines the sensory retina and retinal pigment epithelial layers for surface-level analysis.

Red Laser

Visualizes the RPE to choroid region, revealing deeper structures beneath the retina.

Blue & Infrared Lasers

Support fluorescein and indocyanine green angiography for advanced vascular imaging.
